Seven Game

Rules :

- A player can throw the dices as many times as he wants, until he decides to stop

- If the player throws a 7, his turn stops and his score is reset to 0

- In the end of the game, after 4 turns, the player with the highest score win

- The dices images are not the real numbers, they are here to decorate
